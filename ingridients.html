Our ingredients

  • Garcinia Cambogia

    Garcinia cambogia, a tropical fruit also known as the Malabar tamarind, is a popular weight-loss supplement. It blocks your body's ability to make fat and it puts the brakes on your appetite. It could also help keep blood sugar and cholesterol levels in check

  • Acai Fruit Extract

    Acai contains chemicals that are antioxidants. Antioxidants are thought to protect body cells from the damaging effects of chemical reactions with oxygen. People use acai for osteoarthritis, high cholesterol, erectile dysfunction (ED), weight loss and obesity, “detoxification,” and for improving general health

  • Juniper Berrie

    Juniper berries contain chemicals that might decrease inflammation and gas. It might also be effective in fighting bacteria and viruses. Juniper is used for digestion problems including upset stomach, intestinal gas (flatulence), heartburn, bloating

  • Uva Ursi

    Uva ursi has a history of medicinal use dating back to the 2nd century. It has been widely used as a diuretic, astringent, and antiseptic. Folk medicine around the world has recommended Uva Ursi for nephritis, kidney stones, and chronic cystitis. The herb has also been used as a general tonic for weakened kidneys, liver or pancreas

  • Dandelion Root

    You may consider dandelion as a type of weed, but its root has a long history of therapeutic use. Dandelion is used for loss of appetite, upset stomach, intestinal gas, gallstones, joint pain, muscle aches, eczema, and bruises. In Korean herbal medicine dandelion has been used to improve energy levels and health

  • Buchu Extract

    Buchu leaves contain components that considered to be sweepers or natural detoxifying agents. One of Buchu's most useful properties is its ability to promote the elimination of mucus and uric acid from the kidneys. Buchu Leaf promotes detoxification with its great anti-bacterial properties and helps to improve flow to the kidneys to flush out any toxic wastes, and harmful free radicals

  • Senna Leaf

    Senna is a product approved for use by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration as a laxative. In traditional Chinese medicine, senna leaf removes heat from your colon, helping get rid of waste from old food that has built up there over time
